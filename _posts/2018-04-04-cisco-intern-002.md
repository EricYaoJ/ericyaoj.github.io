- Trace Packet-Input

Only by tracing the right source packet can we get all the information. And it turns out that both `vpp1` and `vpp2` are tracable with `memif-input`. However, wireshark is not able to catch packets sent through `memif` interface. We have to come up with something else to connect `vpp1` and `vpp2` so that we can take a closer look at the packets (and how they are encapsulated/decapsulated).

Another interesting point is that in **VPP**, when using _GRE_, the sender (in our case `vpp1`) always drop the packets silently while from the receiver (in our case `vpp2`) we could see the procedure of en/decapsulation of the packets normally. This remains another task to work on in the future, that is, trying to play with en/decapsulation as well as routing and forwarding packets to some `namespace` out of **VPP**.
